ABOUT

Khozam Group was established in 1960 and has a unique portfolio with an impressive solid track record built over the past six decades. Being inspired by modernist architecture, Khozam Group will feature delightful aesthetic elements to residential and commercial offerings combined. Focus and emphasis will be given to infrastructure, design and cutting-edge techniques that will enhance and give an elegant exterior and interior feel for an overall alluring experience for residents and visitors alike.
